Content: Digital Asset Manager

The Digital Asset Manager is essential for organizing, managing, and distributing digital assets efficiently...

  • Implementing structured metadata and tagging systems to improve searchability.
  • Maintaining brand consistency by ensuring all assets align with organizational guidelines.
  • Streamlining workflows for asset creation, approval, and distribution.
  • Managing rights and permissions to ensure compliance with licensing agreements.

Initial Task: The Digital Asset Manager will focus on setting up a centralized repository for AR-enabled content, ensuring seamless integration with smart glasses functionality.
